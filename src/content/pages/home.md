## Who this is for

Small and mid-sized businesses on the South Shore of Massachusetts — Hingham, Cohasset, Scituate, Duxbury, Hull, Marshfield, and nearby — whose owners want to meet in person, talk plainly, and make good decisions about technology without going through a corporate middleman.

If you run a healthcare practice or service business, I bring extra depth — twelve years of enterprise healthcare IT means I already speak your language. But most of what I do is general small-business technology help, not clinical work.

---

## What "practical" looks like

Twelve-plus years inside enterprise healthcare IT means I've seen how technology fails real people — and how to make it land instead.

I'll tell you when a tool will actually save you hours, and when it won't.

I'll sit with your team for a morning and watch how the work actually happens before I recommend anything.

I'll write a short plan you can read in ten minutes, not a fifty-page deck you'll never open.

I'll build the thing myself, or I'll point you to the person who should — whichever gets you the better result.

And when we're done, you'll know what changed, what to do next, and what to leave alone.
