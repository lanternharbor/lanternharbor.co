## Who this is for

Small and mid-sized businesses on the South Shore of Massachusetts — Hingham, Cohasset, Scituate, Duxbury, Hull, Marshfield, and nearby — whose owners want to meet in person, talk plainly, and make good decisions about technology without going through a corporate middleman.

If you run a healthcare practice or service business, I bring extra depth — twelve years of enterprise healthcare IT means I already speak your language. But most of what I do is general small-business technology help, not clinical work.
